## Observed Patterns

- Usually supports routine auditor ratification unless independence concerns are visible.
- Often votes against executive compensation when dilution, one-time grants, or weak pay-for-performance alignment are present.
- Supports disclosure-oriented shareholder proposals, especially on climate, lobbying, and governance.
- Rarely supports confrontational proposals that appear overly prescriptive or detached from financial materiality.
